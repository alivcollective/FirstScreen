# Health Compass — Global Product Vision

## Mission

Navigate preventable suffering off the map of human experience.

Health Compass is the world's first globally-scaled preventive healthcare navigation platform — designed not to replace physicians, but to ensure every person on Earth can understand their risks, act before disease strikes, and reach the right care at the right time.

---

## The Problem We Solve

### Scale of Preventable Suffering

- **70% of global deaths** are caused by non-communicable diseases (NCDs), most of which are preventable or detectable early
- **40% of cancers** could be prevented or detected at a stage where survival rates exceed 90%
- **50% of people** with diabetes, hypertension, and high cholesterol are undiagnosed
- **$47 trillion** in economic losses projected from NCDs by 2030 (World Economic Forum)
- **5 billion people** lack access to safe, affordable surgical and anesthetic care
- **Health literacy** below 6th grade reading level in populations with highest disease burden

### The Navigation Gap

People don't lack the will to be healthy. They lack:
1. **Risk awareness** — they don't know they're at risk
2. **Screening knowledge** — they don't know what tests to take, when, or where
3. **Trusted guidance** — they can't distinguish reliable from unreliable information
4. **Care pathways** — they don't know how to navigate their local healthcare system
5. **Actionable steps** — they understand the problem but not the next step

---

## The Solution: Health Compass

Health Compass is a healthcare navigation platform — not a diagnostic app, not a telemedicine service, not a health tracker. It is the GPS layer for preventive healthcare.

### Core Analogy: Google Maps for Your Health

Just as Google Maps:
- Shows you where you are
- Reveals what's nearby
- Calculates the optimal route
- Updates in real time
- Works in every country

Health Compass does the same for healthcare:
- Reveals your personal risk landscape
- Shows available preventive services
- Creates your personalized care pathway
- Updates as guidelines and your health evolve
- Works across every healthcare system on Earth

## The Platform Architecture Pillars

### 1. Disease Intelligence Network
A living, evidence-graded knowledge base covering 10,000+ conditions, powered by medical literature, WHO guidelines, and country-specific clinical standards. Every fact sourced, versioned, and reviewed.

### 2. Symptom Navigation Engine
Not a symptom checker. A **healthcare literacy tool** that transforms vague concerns into structured understanding — helping users know when to act, what to ask, and where to go. Zero diagnostic claims.

### 3. Preventive Screening Planner
Personalized screening timelines based on age, sex, family history, lifestyle, and country-specific guidelines. Syncs with provider networks. Sends reminders. Closes the screening gap.

### 4. Risk Assessment Platform
Validated risk calculators for:
- Cardiovascular disease (Framingham, QRISK3, WHO CVD Risk)
- Type 2 Diabetes (ADA, FINDRISC)
- Cancer (BCRAT, PCPTRC, NLST criteria)
- Mental health (PHQ-9, GAD-7, validated instruments)
- Chronic kidney disease, COPD, osteoporosis

All calculators produce **actionable outputs**, not just scores.

### 5. Health Literacy Academy
Evidence-based health education. Courses. Videos. Infographics. Translated into 100+ languages. Accredited by medical institutions. Shareable by public health systems.

### 6. Healthcare Provider Network
A searchable, vetted directory of hospitals, clinics, specialists, and screening centers. Appointment integration. Quality ratings. Coverage verification. Available in every partner country.

### 7. Insurance & Prevention Marketplace
Prevention-linked insurance products. Employer wellness programs. Screening packages. Built on the insight that prevention is the most cost-effective insurance product ever created.

### 8. AI Health Education Assistant
A conversational AI that answers health questions using only evidence-based sources. Never diagnoses. Always educates. Always routes to care when needed. Transparent about what it is.

### 9. Population Health Analytics
Anonymized, consented aggregate data that powers public health intelligence. Screening rates. Risk factor prevalence. Healthcare access gaps. Sold to health ministries, researchers, insurers.

### 10. Research Collaboration Platform
The connective tissue between patients, providers, public health systems, and researchers. IRB-compliant. Privacy-preserving. Accelerating the translation of research into practice.
