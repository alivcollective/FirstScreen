# Thailand Launch Strategy

## Why Thailand First

### Strategic Rationale

Thailand is the ideal Phase 1 market for Health Compass:

| Factor | Thailand Reality |
|--------|-----------------|
| Digital readiness | 85% smartphone penetration, 77% internet usage |
| Health awareness | Strong post-COVID health consciousness |
| NCD burden | #1 cause of death; 35% population at risk |
| Healthcare infrastructure | Universal Coverage Scheme (30-baht scheme) + private sector |
| English proficiency | High among educated urban population |
| Regulatory environment | Progressive digital health policy |
| Medical tourism hub | World-class private hospitals seeking digital partnerships |
| Population size | 71 million — meaningful scale, manageable complexity |

### Thailand's Health Crisis

- **Non-communicable diseases** cause 71% of all deaths
- **Diabetes** affects 7.4 million Thais (11% of adults); 40% undiagnosed
- **Hypertension** affects 13 million; 50% uncontrolled
- **Cancer** — cervical, liver, colorectal, breast among top killers; screening rates below 30%
- **Cardiovascular disease** — leading cause of death, largely preventable
- **Mental health** — 1 in 7 Thais affected; massive treatment gap

## Thailand-Specific Product Requirements

### Language & Culture
- **Thai language first** — All core UX in Thai
- Thai date system support (Buddhist Era calendar)
- Thai address formats
- Thai ID card integration option
- Right-to-left consideration not needed for Thai, but needed for future Arabic markets — build infrastructure now

### Healthcare System Integration
- **Universal Coverage Scheme (UCS)** — 48M beneficiaries
- **Social Security Scheme (SSS)** — 15M beneficiaries
- **Civil Servant Medical Benefit Scheme (CSMBS)** — 5M beneficiaries
- **Private insurance** integration
- Thai hospital accreditation (HA Thailand) display

### Regulatory Compliance
- **PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act)** — Effective since 2022
- **Medical Device Act** — Avoid classification as medical device
- **Healthcare Information Standards** — Align with MOPH standards
- Legal structure: Thai company (100% foreign ownership possible in tech sector via BOI)

### Thai-Specific Disease Focus
**Priority Conditions for Thailand Launch:**
1. Type 2 Diabetes (highest population impact)
2. Hypertension & Cardiovascular Disease
3. Cervical Cancer (high incidence, highly preventable)
4. Liver Cancer (HBV/HCV related — endemic)
5. Colorectal Cancer
6. Breast Cancer
7. Dengue Fever (seasonal awareness)
8. Thalassemia (genetic — high Thai prevalence)
9. Chronic Kidney Disease
10. Depression & Anxiety

### Screening Guidelines — Thailand
Align with:
- MOPH National Screening Program
- NHSO Preventive Care Package
- Thai Society of Internal Medicine
- Royal College of Physicians of Thailand
- Thai Cardiovascular Society
