# Business Model & Revenue Strategy
# Health Compass

---

## Core Business Model Philosophy

**Prevention is the most cost-effective health intervention ever created.**

Health Compass monetizes this truth in multiple ways — none of which compromise user trust or our mission. Revenue follows trust. Trust follows value. Value follows better health outcomes.

**Sacred Boundaries:**
- Never sell user health data
- Never allow advertising to influence health content
- Never gate critical health information behind paywalls
- Never allow revenue to compromise medical accuracy

---

## Revenue Streams

### Stream 1: Premium Consumer Subscriptions

**Product:** Health Compass Premium

**Features:**
- Unlimited risk assessments + history
- Full screening planner with reminders
- Appointment booking integration
- AI Health Guide (extended sessions)
- Unlimited course access (Academy)
- Family profiles (up to 5 members)
- Priority customer support

**Pricing (Thailand Launch):**
- Monthly: ฿299/month (~$8.50 USD)
- Annual: ฿2,399/year (~$68 USD) — 33% discount
- Family: ฿499/month (~$14 USD) — up to 5 members

**Global Pricing Principle:** Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) adjustments per country

**TAM (Thailand):** 71M population × 15% addressable × ฿299/month = ฿38B/year theoretical maximum

**Year 3 Target:** 100,000 paid subscribers → ฿358M/year

---

### Stream 2: Corporate Wellness

**Product:** Health Compass for Enterprise

**Pitch:** Reduce sick days, lower group insurance premiums, demonstrate ESG health commitment, satisfy occupational health requirements.

**Package:**
- Employee Health Dashboard
- Group risk assessments
- Aggregate (anonymized) workforce health reports
- Customized screening campaigns
- Health education campaigns
- Integration with HR systems
- Executive health reporting

**Pricing (Thailand):**
- SME (50–200 employees): ฿500/employee/year
- Mid-market (200–1,000 employees): ฿450/employee/year
- Enterprise (1,000+): ฿380/employee/year

**Target Companies:** CP Group, PTT, SCB, Kasikorn Bank, Thai Airways, Central Group, True Corporation

**Year 3 Target:** 50 corporate clients (avg 500 employees) → ฿100M/year

---

### Stream 3: Hospital & Provider Network

**Product:** Health Compass Provider Listings + Integration

**Products:**
1. **Enhanced Listing** — Verified badge, detailed profile, featured placement: ฿30,000–80,000/year
2. **Appointment Integration** — Deep-link or embedded booking: Revenue share (10% of attributed bookings)
3. **Screening Packages** — Health Compass-branded screening bundles sold through provider: 15% platform fee
4. **Content Partnership** — Hospital-branded educational content: ฿200,000–500,000/campaign

**Year 3 Target:** 200 provider partners → ฿50M/year

---

### Stream 4: Insurance Marketplace

**Product:** Prevention-linked insurance marketplace

**Model:**
- Insurance products listed on marketplace
- User matches with appropriate products after risk assessment
- Affiliate commission: 15–25% of first year premium on referred policies
- Embedded insurance: Real-time insurance quote during risk assessment

**Thailand Market:**
- AIA Thailand, Krungthai-AXA, FWD, Bangkok Insurance, Muang Thai Insurance
- Average commission: ฿1,500–3,000 per referred policy

**Unique Value:** Risk-aware users who have just completed a risk assessment are the highest-quality insurance leads in Thailand.

**Year 3 Target:** 50,000 referred policies at ฿2,000 avg commission → ฿100M/year

---

### Stream 5: Population Health Analytics

**Product:** Aggregate health intelligence for governments, insurers, researchers

**Customers:**
- Health ministries (national screening rates, risk factor prevalence)
- Actuarial teams (insurance pricing intelligence)
- Public health researchers (consented, IRB-approved datasets)
- Pharmaceutical companies (disease burden insights — aggregate only)

**Products:**
1. **Standard Reports** — Annual country health burden report: ฿200,000–1,000,000/country
2. **API Access** — Real-time aggregate metrics: ฿500,000–2,000,000/year
3. **Custom Research** — Commissioned population health studies: ฿1,000,000–5,000,000/study
4. **Government Contracts** — National screening program monitoring: ฿5,000,000–50,000,000/contract

**Privacy Guarantee:** Only k-anonymized aggregates (k≥100). No individual-level data ever sold.

---

### Stream 6: Sponsored Health Education

**Product:** Pharmaceutical/NGO-sponsored educational content

**Model:**
- Pharma companies sponsor disease awareness content (NOT product advertising)
- Content is independently produced and medically reviewed
- Sponsor logo shown as "educational supporter" — not as content author
- Full editorial independence maintained
- No sponsored content can advocate for specific products

**Clear Labeling:** "This educational content was produced by Health Compass with funding support from [Sponsor]. Health Compass maintains full editorial control."

**Pricing:** ฿500,000–3,000,000 per major campaign/condition

---

### Stream 7: Research Tools & API

**Product:** Developer API and research data products

**API Products:**
- Disease Intelligence API: $99–999/month
- Screening Guidelines API: $199–1,999/month
- Risk Calculator API: $299–2,999/month
- Search API: $99–999/month

**Research Platform:**
- Data access for IRB-approved studies
- Institutional licensing
